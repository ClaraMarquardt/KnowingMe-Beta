You Are What You Write

Every time you write an email, you’re creating a piece of data. One email - one piece of data - doesn’t tell us much. But you probably write hundreds in a week. You have thousands of saved emails that you’ve sent and received. What if we looked at all those emails, combed through them to find patterns. What would we find?

Using our state-of-the-art analytical techniques, we review your emails and generate findings you’d never know we could uncover. We compare how you communicate with different groups, finding patterns in the way you write and construct your emails. We’re so excited to guide you on a personal journey that will help you unlock insights that you almost-but-didn’t-quite know about yourself.
