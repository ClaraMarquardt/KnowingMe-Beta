On the next screen, we'll show you sentiment scores for a few different categories of email:

 Average Positivity
The average positivity of all of your sent and received emails.

 Positivity Imbalance
The ratio between the positivity of the emails you sent and the positivity of the emails you responded to. A positivity imbalance greater than 1 shows that you're more 'positive' to others than they are to you.
